Overview

Austin Area Arts is a non-profit arts organization that offers a variety of classes hosted by local artists, an annual art festival to showcase local creative talent and access to a variety of popular films and performances at their historic theater.

Their goal is to provide engaging and diverse arts-related activities and make community members feel excited at the opportunity to learn and experience new forms of art.

The Challenge

The existing website lacks a streamlined and intuitive user flow for the local community to find events, view class information, or inquire for details about the festival.

Due to the absence of understandable navigational patterns, users are not able to effectively engage with the site.
