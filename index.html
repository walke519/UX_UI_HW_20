About Me

I’m Lauren (they/them) and I’m a designer based in Minneapolis, MN. I’m inspired by the impact that design has on our lives; the conversations it sparks, the emotions it can evoke. It’s an inherent part of our lives, we interact with it everyday, and it can make an impact in the subtlest ways. I want to be a part of that process and be challenged to learn and grow as a designer in each role that I take.
